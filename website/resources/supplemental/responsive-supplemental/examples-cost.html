Total Cost of Major Natural Disasters

This visualization is based on the New York Times article The Places in the U.S. Where Disaster Strikes Again and Again. This visualization exhibits a number of interesting changes between the desktop and mobile visualization, primarily around the annotation strategy and title placement.

For each visualization, you can use the three dots to (1) save the image, (2) view the Vega-Lite spec, and (3) see the result in the Online Vega Editor. You can also view the complete video walkthrough of this example.
